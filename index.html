Questions? We have answers.

Is there any technical setup?

No, just go to the app, sign up and make a profile. You can download the mobile apps to your device but with a profile Venio will work on any devices you have the app.

How do my clients get it?

Your clients are able to sign-up for the Venio application on the web and on the iPhone through the Appstore.

Can I use my own recipes?

Yes, anyone is able to add recipes to the Venio Recipe Database. To do this, we created a Recipe Input Form. After a recipe is entered, it will then be curated and added to the database if it passes our Venio score health test.

Is personal information protected?

Yes, all information on the Venio platform is encrypted and up to rigorous industry standards.
